TMS offers a PCB five stage Reverse Engineering service:

Stage 1. Carry out component identification/values using capacitance, inductance, resistance meters, oscilloscope, electronic microscope and X-Ray equipment for analysis and verification.

Stage 2. Produce a Bill of Materials including manufacturers part references, availability and (where necessary) equivalent or improved parts.

Stage 3. (If required) Download and disassemble machine code from EPROM/PIC devices using component specific reader equipment and write code to transportable storage media.

Stage 4. Reverse engineer the Printed Circuit Board and produce the circuit diagram, PCB layout and legends for the production Gerber files.

Stage 5. Produce a working prototype from the acquired reverse engineering information.

TH to SMD Conversions

TMS also offer a TH (through hole) to SMD design service, ideal for updating old designs to make them more cost effective.
