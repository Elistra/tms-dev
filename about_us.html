About Us

Team Manufacturing Services was established over 25 years ago in Wokingham, as a cable assembly company trading under the name Robinson's Computer Technical Services.

In 1993 the trading name was changed to Robinson's Direct and with the introduction of a new business partner in 2005 the trading name was changed to Team Manufacturing Services (TEAM being the combined initials of the two senior partners at the time!)

TMS then expanded it's manufacturing to include full turnkey PCB assembly including SMT and TH in an environmentally clean air and full ESD protected  working environment.

Continuing the expansion policy, the scope of work now includes full mechanical assembly and advanced rework/repair services.
