Full Box Build Services

TMS can source all your components, enclosures, metal-work and fixings.

Combined with the manufacturing of the PCB's and cables we have the facilities to fully assemble and test the completed units. We can also design and print adhesive vinyl labels for panel fascias.

The customer can provide any or all of the components as free issued items if desired.

Box builds have previously included 19" rack assemblies, ruggedised cases, power distribution and mobiles communications systems.

TMS has a no minimum order quantity policy.

Fixings Reliability

All assemblies are tightened using precision torque controlled tooling to ensure the optimum reliability of fixings.

Tightening torque figures are recorded for each production batch and are copied to the 'Client Area' for customer inspection.
