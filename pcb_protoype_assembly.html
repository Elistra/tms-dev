Prototyping

TMS can offer a fast and cost effective PCB prototyping service by producing the track photo positives and carrying out the PCB board exposure and etching processes in-house.
We have produced a custom electronic design through to a working PCB prototype assembly including bespoke cables within 4 hours of the Customer's original request.

Component to Pad Verification

At TMS we understand the value of the prototype for design verification and also in production planning.
Using the prototype, we work closely with the customer detailing all aspects of the assembly including building a 2D model of the SMT assembly to ensure all component to pad layouts are correct.
The component to pad production verification service we provide as standard prevents potentially costly modifications to PCBs following final assembly.
