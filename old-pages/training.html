TMS IPC-A-610E Acceptability of Electronic Assemblies
Certified IPC Specialist (CIS) Training Course

Run by Mr Daniel Ashworth (CIT) - TMS Senior Partner, Certified IPC Trainer (Cert. No.610-T-34000340) - Trainer since 2008.

Why choose TMS training?

  • A maximum of two students on any training course for greater learning efficiency.

  • Training is carried out in a fully equipped PCB lab environment with production equipment to help reinforce the learning process.

  • All nine training modules are covered.

  • The Lecturer has over 19 years of PCB hands-on production experience (with 100% candidate pass rate to-date).

  • Fully air conditioned training area with natural lighting.

  • Free off-road and secure parking.

  • Refreshments and light lunches included.

  • IPC-A-610E Key notes, stationary, examination papers and pass Certificate included.

Course Overview

Module 1 Introduction/IPC Professional Policies and Procedures
Module 2 Foreword, Applicable Documents and Handling
Module 3 Hardware Installation
Module 4 Soldering
Module 5 Terminal Connections
Module 6 Through-Hole Technology including TH Jumper Wires
Module 7 Surface Mount Assemblies including SMT Jumper Wires
Module 8 Component Damage, Printed Circuit Boards and Assemblies
Module 9 Solderless Wire Wrap

The IPC-A-610E CIS training is a three day course and runs from Monday to Wednesday of selected weeks during the year at TMS.
From experience, we have found June to September to be the favoured time of year to run the courses.
However, we can arrange unscheduled courses on request provided there are sufficient candidates (minimum of two).
