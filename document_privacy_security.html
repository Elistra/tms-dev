Privacy Policy

TMS takes our customers privacy very seriously. We will ensure that any personal or business information you entrust with us remains safe and secure. We will not share your personal or business information with other people or businesses without your prior consent. We do not sell, rent or exchange your personal or business information with any third party for commercial reasons. We are registered under the Data Protection Act 1998 and fully comply with the Act and related legislation. Your design and manufacturing information is extremely safe with us because of our strict data security policy which includes:

  • Non-Disclosure Agreements for all staff
  • Need to Know information control
  • Senior Partner signatories to HM Government Official Secrets Act 1989
  • ISO 27002 implementation for our computers and networks
  • Physically separated LAN and WAN communications infrastructure
  • LAN accessed manufacturing only via single AV regulated biometrically secured computer
  • VPN secure data transfer
  • 256bit Encrypted data storage
  • Locked Fireproof and Waterproof data storage
  • 24hr attended on-site security with CCTV and PIR/Microwave coverage
  • PSTN plus SIM autodialing security alarm system
  • Completely fenced offices and manufacturing buildings including motorised gate with intercom controlled access

All this protection comes as standard and at no additional cost. We can also offer Patent alternative design protection services including component ID removal, epoxy potting, anti-X-ray and self-destructing anti tamper devices.
